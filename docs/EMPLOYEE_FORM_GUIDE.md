## Tips for Submitting

1. **Required fields** are marked with a red asterisk (*) - these must be filled before submitting
2. **Review before submit** - Click "Save Report" to see a preview of all your data
3. **Check your numbers** - Ensure all monetary values are in USD
4. **One report per brand** - Submit separate reports for each brand you manage
